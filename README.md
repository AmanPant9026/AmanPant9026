## 🚀 Things I've Built

**📄 Agentic Document Intelligence** &nbsp;·&nbsp; *Bosch · 2025–Present*

End-to-end agentic pipeline using **LangChain + GLM-OCR + QWEN** to extract structured JSON from complex, table-heavy PDFs. Config-driven orchestration scales across document types — hit **88.98% recall** on shipping-bill benchmarks, on par with GPT.

---

**📊 Synthetic Financial Data — American Express** &nbsp;·&nbsp; *Jun 2025–Present*

Generating privacy-preserving synthetic data across Credit Default, Philadelphia Payments, and Fund Holdings datasets. Benchmarking **CTGAN, TVAE, FinDiff & TabSyn** on fidelity (Shape, Trend), utility (C2ST), and privacy (α-Precision, β-Recall, DCR) — diffusion models leading at **95.89 C2ST**.

---

**🧠 Brain Tumor Progression** &nbsp;·&nbsp; *Jan–Jun 2025*

Automated **BraTS-Pro pipeline** for longitudinal MRI tumour progression analysis. Trained a **Siamese 3D-CNN** achieving **88% accuracy & 90% recall** — designed to assist clinicians with reliable, reproducible assessments over time.

---

**🎙️ Voice of the Nation — Language Identification** &nbsp;·&nbsp; *Apr 2025*

10-language speech classifier using **Wav2Vec XLS-R 300M + OpenL3** embeddings and Orthogonal Projection Loss → **82% accuracy**. Secured **🥈 2nd place among 300+ teams** at an HCL AI Hackathon @ IIT Mandi.

---

**⚡ Hybrid Cache Replacement Policy** &nbsp;·&nbsp; *Oct–Dec 2024*

Implemented a smart block-replacement policy in **MacSIM** for hybrid volatile/non-volatile cache systems. Achieved **45% reduction in power consumption** vs SRAM-only caches — a clean systems-level win.
